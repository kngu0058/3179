Impact of COVID-19

The impact of COVID-19 has led to dramatic losses in all parts of the world including economic, social, and environmental detriments. It was also become a challenge to maintain stability in all countries. Socially, millions of people have fallen into extreme poverty due to the pandemic while half of the world’s enterprises face risk of financial hardships. As a result, we must consider our actions in a world of uncertainty.

Spread of the Disease

Transmission of coronavirus can be through the air that within short range. The particles can be spread in poorly ventilated indoor areas where people are within distance of each other for long periods of time. Through this, the virus is able to infect the person through their eyes, nose or mouth. It is then recommended to avoid crowded places, close-contact settings and confined and enclosed spaces to reduce the spread of COVID-19.

Fatality
